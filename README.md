# CyberShield
A web-based cybercrime reporting platform that connects victims with authorities that is built with AI support, case tracking, and an authority dashboard.
-> Full Feature Description

1. 🏠 Landing Page
The main homepage that introduces CyberShield. It explains what the platform does, shows stats (reports filed, response rate), lists the types of crimes you can report, and has a clear "File a Report" button. First impression for any visitor.

2. 📋 Report Form
The core feature. A victim fills out a structured form — they pick the type of crime, severity level, which platform it happened on, describe the incident, and optionally upload screenshots as evidence. They can also choose to stay anonymous. On submit, they get a unique Tracking ID.

3. 🤖 AI Support Chat
A chat widget (bottom corner of the screen) powered by AI. A user can type something like "someone is blackmailing me on Instagram" and the AI will help them understand what kind of crime it is, what information to include in the report, and calm them down if they're distressed. Think of it as a 24/7 guided assistant.

4. 🏛️ Authority Dashboard
A separate, password-protected panel for cybercrime officers. They can log in and see all submitted reports in a table — filter by type, severity, or status. They can mark a case as Under Review, Resolved, or Rejected, and add internal notes. Basically the backend management panel for authorities.

5. 🔍 Report Tracking Page
A simple page where a victim enters their Tracking ID (given after submission) and can see the current status of their case — Submitted → Under Review → Resolved. Keeps victims informed without needing to contact anyone.

6. 📞 Emergency Helpline Section
A section on the website listing official Pakistani cybercrime helpline numbers, FIA contacts, and emergency resources. So if someone needs immediate help, they know who to call right away.

7. ⚖️ Know Your Rights Page
An informational page explaining Pakistani cyber laws in simple language — what counts as a cybercrime legally, what punishments exist, and what rights victims have. Empowers users with knowledge.

8. 📊 Live Statistics Dashboard
A visual dashboard showing real-time crime stats — how many reports per category, which platforms are most reported, severity breakdown — shown as charts and graphs. Makes the platform feel credible and data-driven.
